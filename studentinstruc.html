    Student Instructions

    Provide students with the personal login credentials.

    • For quicker and easier access to the website you may want to bookmark the website (elicitingmathmeaning.edc.org) or write the URL on the board for students.

    Prior to Students Logging Into The Assessment

    • Students will see the assessment problems as soon as they login.

    • Read the instructions to students before they login. These instructions can be found on the Teacher Instruction page.

    After Reading The Instructions To Students

    • Students should go to the website using a bookmark or typing in the URL: elicitingmathmeaning.edc.org

    • Students should enter the personal login credentials they have each been given.

    • Students should click the Login button once their credentials have been entered.

    • Students will see the assessment problems as soon as they login.

    • When students have completed the assessment, they should click the Submit button.

    • Students who do not finish the assessment in the allotted time the can click the Finish Later button.

    • As students click the Finish Later button or the Submit button they will be logged out of the assessment.
